Round 2 Rules

Round 2 is a knock out round. Each game has to have a winner. In the event of a draw there will be extra time. At the end of extra time if there is no winner there will be penalties!

For our competition a win on penalties counts as an extra goal. ie Spain 2 - France 2. France win on penalties. We will treat is as Spain 2 France 3!

Please get the form back as soon as you can and GOOD LUCK!
